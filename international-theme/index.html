International President's Theme

We Serve Through Diversity

Creating harmony through service with 102nd International President, Dr. Jung-Yul Choi

 

As a Lion for over 40 years, International President Choi leads by example. His career and life have been defned by a passionate work ethic, along with a dedication to bridging diferences, connecting people and embracing diversity to create harmony for all.

 

“If you work harder than you’re expected to, extraordinary results will not be far behind.”

The Many Ways We Serve

Specialty Clubs Collapse

While Lions serve a wide range of needs in their communities, more and more clubs are forming around a specifc area of interest, a single cause or common background. Specialty clubs allow people to join a community that is specifc to their situation and interests—growing membership and helping our organization do even greater good. See the diference at lionsclubs.org/SpecialtyClubs.

Members of all Ages Expand

Our members represent every culture, background and generation. This year, we can strengthen our clubs—for today and tomorrow—by inviting young people and seniors to join us in service. This multi-generational approach is critical to unifying people, leveraging skills and experience, and making clubs a welcoming place for all. Strengthen your membership at lionsclubs.org/InviteMembers.

Global Causes Expand

Our new global causes give Lions around the world new opportunities to unite so we can address some of the most pressing issues facing humanity. This year, we’re encouraging all clubs to expand their service and use the resources available to them to make an even bigger diference in their communities. Find your cause at lionsclubs.org/GlobalCauses.

Service Journey Collapse

Learn, Discover, Act, Celebrate. These are the four steps of our new Service Journey, a comprehensive way for Lions to increase their service impact through access to resources supporting our global causes. Start your journey at lionsclubs.org/ServiceJourney.

Supporting Our Foundation Expand

Campaign 100 is a three-year efort by Lions Clubs International Foundation to raise $US300 million, with the goal of empowering the service of Lions around the world. As we enter the second year of the campaign, we want to keep our momentum going by encouraging everyone to give what they can. Together, we can bring long-term change and lasting hope to the communities that need us most. Support our Foundation at lcif.org/Donate.
