FAQs for e-voting process - District 3231-A3

STEP 1 - 15th April, 2020 to 30th April, 2020

Who can register delegates on MyLCI?

Club President / Secretary can register delegates on My LCI

Where to go to register delegates on MyLCI?

Visit MyLCI, Select convention, thereafter Select Lions District 3231A3 Third Annual convention dated 05/24/2020

What is the process to get confirmation numbers for registered delegates?

Select delegate option, Add delegate menu ;  Register all delegate & Obtain delegate confirmation number and pass on the delegate confirmation number to the delegate to be filled in Annexure VIII.

Who can file annexure VII & how?

  1. Club President/ Secretary  to submit the  list of authorised club delegates  on club letterhead  as per Annexure VII (Click here to download).
  2. In case club president / secretary do not have the club letterhead in such cases the details to be submitted from club Presidents registered email id.  
  3. Signatures on Annexure VII are not mandatory if email is sent from the registered Email id of the President. But if  sent  on whats app it should be signed by the President.
  4. Email id [email protected] or Whats App number 7710032007.

Where can we find the link to submit delegates data for E-voting?

  1. On the district website lions3231a3.org
  2. Email
  3. Whats App

Can the delegates submit their data themselves?

Yes

Where the delegate can submit details as per annexure VIII?

Click the link https://lionsclubs3231a3.evote.co.in/registration/registration_form.php

Is Annexure VIII is lengthy and complicated?

No, Annexure VIII is simple and it takes only 2 minutes to fill all the fields.

  1. Select club  
  2. Name of delegate 
  3. Membership no.,  
  4. Delegate confirmation number as per MyLCI, 
  5. Date of birth, 
  6. Gender, 
  7. Mobile number, 
  8. Email Id, 
  9. Residence address, 
  10. Office address, 
  11. Upload photo (photo below 2mb) 
  12. Photo id Proof to  match  photo and data on MyLci, (at least one address must match with my LCI). In case of district directory write the page number. 
  13. Click to confirm the data 
  14. Enter the Captcha  and 
  15. Submit.

NOTE :- Please keep all the data, photo , Photo ID proof handy for filling all the fields. 

On submission you will get the reference number and confirmation on your email ( Please check your inbox or spam ).

Which are the important personal data to be checked for delegates at MyLCI?

  1. Mobile number
  2. Email id
  3. Address
  4. Date of Birth
