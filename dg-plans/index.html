District Governor's Plans & Policies

DISTRICT GOVERNOR's FOCUS ACTIVITIES

  1. Setting up a 7-Storey Multispecialty Trauma Care ‘LIONS CHARITABLE HOSPITAL’ in Satellite area within the
    jurisdiction of our Lions District
  2. Propose to set up 25 need based Permanent Projects in the form of Charitable Diagnostic Centres, preferably in
    slum areas A diagnostic centre can be Eye clinic, Diabetes Clinic of even Blood Sample Collection Centre. A typical centre may be admeasuring about 250-300sq.ft. or larger. ‘A Club can come up with a desire plan to set up such a clinic. We shall provide all the technical assistance to make your intention, a reality’
  3. Our aim is to donate and install 25 or more Dialysis Machines or to set up 3 New Dialysis Centres across our District
    area. ‘Once again, we shall provide guidance to the clubs for the noble cause’
  4. We invite and motivate clubs to upgrade and modernize all existing Lions Hospitals and Diagnostic Centres
  5. Propose to motivate and support school dropouts to return back for formal education
  6. Design and/or facilitate skill development programs to create skilled personnel
  7. Support education for underprivileged through
    • Adoption of Municipal Schools (at least 10)
    • Adoption of Night Schools (at least 10)
  8. Support for Senior Citizen Welfare and Old Age Homes
  9. A permanent BHOJAN SHALA, benefitting patients & their relatives coming from out station to Mumbai for
    treatment in major Government Hospitals
  10. Mediclaim insurance policies for underprivileged
  11. Create as many Check Dams as we can. They would contribute to more and more Water conservation. Water is
    always the most precious resource, but we realize it only when it becomes scarce!
  12. Lions Quest implementation for 20,000 plus students. ‘With a plunge taken by social media in our country, there is
    increasing need for social and emotional learning’
  13. Creating awareness and guidance on how to take benefit from Government's various welfare schemes (such as
    National Social Assistance Scheme, Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana, etc.)
  14. We, the Lions are pillars of society and we believe in GIVING WITHOUT EXPECTATION. Therefore, we introduce
    Privilege Schemes for the Lions. They include
    • Health check-up scheme for LIONS and their families
    • Pathology Lab tests at discounted rates for LIONS and their families
    • Group Insurance scheme for LIONS and their families This list of privilege benefits is incomplete and more benefits will be added in course of time
